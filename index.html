TRISCCON 2017
 

The 2020 Trivandrum School on Communications, Coding, and Networking (TRISCCON-2020) with the theme “Theory and Practice of Machine Learning” has the following broad objectives for its participants:

  • understand the basics of linear algebra, probability, and the foundational concepts of convex optimization; at the end of the workshop the participants will have sufficient theoretical base to study algorithms tailored for optimization

  • exposure to the theoretical basis of three important streams of machine learning – supervised learning, unsupervised learning and reinforcement learning.

  • hands-on experience with machine learning algorithms in the popular Python machine learning stack in several sessions

  • understand how machine learning is applied to various problems in academia and industry in the fields of communication, computer vision, and space applications.
