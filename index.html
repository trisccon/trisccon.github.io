The 2017 Trivandrum School on Communications, Coding, and Networking (TRISCCON-2017) aims to

  • give a detailed introduction to the fundamental concepts in the field of communication, coding, and networking - such as linear algebra, probability, statistics, and estimation.

  • provide an opportunity for participating faculty members (under TEQIP-II) to learn pedagogically sound methods to teach and demonstrate fundamental concepts in these areas to their students

  • show how the fundamental concepts are extended for use in cutting edge research topics, thereby empowering participants in carrying out their research projects

  • provide an opportunity for participants to interact with and be motivated and mentored by the expert speakers
